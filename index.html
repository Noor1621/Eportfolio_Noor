Research Engagements

  • Gender-Based Insights into Climate Change Effects, Vulnerability and Adaptive Responses in Agriculture, Lalitpur Nepal

Research Focus

My research examines snow cover dynamics in the Austrian Alps, focusing on: Snow Cover Variability and Long-Term Trends: Analyzing changes in snow cover extent and duration across different seasons and years. Snow-Albedo Feedback and Regional Climate Impact: Investigating how variations in snow cover influence albedo, surface temperature, and broader climatic conditions. Temperature and Precipitation Trends: Assessing the relationship between snow cover changes and climate variables, including rising temperatures, shifting precipitation patterns, and extreme weather events.

Research Focus

My research investigates the impact of climate change on smallholder farmers, with a particular focus on: Perceived Impacts of Climate Change on Agriculture: Exploring how smallholder farmers perceive the effects of climate change on their farming practices. Factors Influencing Farmers' Perceptions: Identifying the key factors that shape farmers' views on the impacts of climate change. Climate Change Adaptation Practices: Examining the adaptation strategies adopted by farmers to mitigate the effects of climate change. Determinants of Adaptation Practice Adoption: Analyzing the factors that influence the adoption of different climate change adaptation practices among farmers.

Research Focus

My research investigates the gender-specific vulnerabilities within agricultural practices due to climate change impacts. It explores the differences in how men and women perceive and respond to climate change, particularly in relation to autonomous adaptation strategies in farming. Additionally, the study examines gender-based disparities in participation and engagement in climate change adaptation initiatives, aiming to understand how gender influences the adoption of strategies that mitigate the effects of climate change on agricultural communities. By addressing these gendered aspects, the research seeks to inform more inclusive and effective climate adaptation policies and practices.
